# 🌴 GoaGenie — AI-Powered Smart Travel Assistant

GoaGenie is a smart tourism platform that helps travelers plan their Goa trip intelligently using AI, real-time data, and local insights. Instead of overwhelming users with scattered information, it transforms inputs into actionable travel decisions.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 🤖 AI-Powered Itinerary Generator

Generate personalized, day-wise travel plans based on your budget, trip duration, and preferences using advanced AI.

---

### ⚠️ Smart Travel Guidance (What to Avoid)

GoaGenie doesn’t just suggest what to do — it warns users about poor decisions like visiting places at the wrong time, unrealistic schedules, or bad weather conditions.

---

### 🎤 Voice-Based Local Translator

Speak naturally in English or Hindi and get instant translations with audio output, helping tourists communicate easily with locals.

---

### ⛅ Real-Time Weather Intelligence

Get live weather updates and smart travel suggestions based on conditions like rain, heat, or storms.

---

### 📍 Curated Spot Explorer

Discover beaches, cafes, forts, and hidden gems with structured, easy-to-understand information.

---

### 🗺️ Navigation & Location Support

Integrated maps and location tracking to help users navigate efficiently during their trip.

---

## 🧠 What Makes GoaGenie Different

Unlike traditional travel apps that only provide information, GoaGenie focuses on **decision-making** — helping users plan smarter, avoid mistakes, and improve their overall travel experience.
